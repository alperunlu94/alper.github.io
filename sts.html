Sütaş Steam Boiler and Auxiliary Equipment Project: Solutions Compatible with Customer Needs

In today's industry, energy efficiency and sustainability play a critical role in the success of businesses. This project for Sütaş Dairy Products Inc. aims to meet the company's steam boiler and auxiliary equipment needs.

Project Overview

The main goal of this project is to design and install a high-efficiency and eco-friendly steam boiler system for use in Sütaş's production processes. The project aims to reduce the company's energy consumption, thereby lowering costs and minimizing environmental impact.

Project Scope

Within the scope of the project, Scotch type steam boilers and various auxiliary equipment have been customized according to Sütaş's specific needs. The boilers are designed to operate with natural gas and diesel fuel, and are manufactured in compliance with energy efficiency and environmental standards.

Auxiliary Equipment

The auxiliary equipment includes boiler feed pumps, automation systems, burners, economizer devices, stainless steel chimneys and flue ducts, meters, deaerator systems, steam collectors and fittings, PLC automation and monitoring systems, diesel tank and equipment, plate heat exchanger groups, and blowdown heat recovery systems.

Installation and Commissioning

The project also covers all installation, testing, and commissioning processes necessary for the efficient operation of the boiler rooms. An energy efficiency monitoring system will enable the real-time tracking of the boiler's efficiency values, facilitating continuous improvement.
